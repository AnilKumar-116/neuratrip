Why Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple?

Kashi Vishwanath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, is the epitome of Hindu spirituality. Situated in the holy city of Varanasi, it draws pilgrims seeking blessings from Lord Shiva and connection to ancient traditions.

When to Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple?

The ideal months to visit are October to March, when the weather is pleasant. Maha Shivratri is the most celebrated festival, attracting thousands of devotees for night-long prayers and festivities.

Route to Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Varanasi is well connected by air, rail, and road. Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport is 26 km away. Varanasi Junction railway station links the city with major Indian cities. Local transport is available to reach the temple easily.
