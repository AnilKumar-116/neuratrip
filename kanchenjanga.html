🌄 Why Visit Kanchenjunga?

🏔️ Third Highest Peak

Kanchenjunga stands tall at 8,586m. A pilgrimage, not a tourist trap.

🧘 Sacred Land

Locals worship the mountain. Every step is a spiritual journey.

🐾 Rare Wildlife

Red pandas. Snow leopards. Glacial rivers. Unspoiled beauty awaits.

🚀 How to Visit

📍 Choose Your Route

North Base Camp (Pangpema) or South Base Camp (Oktang). Or do both.

🗓️ Best Time

Visit in Spring (Mar–May) or Autumn (Sep–Nov) for clear skies & trails.

🛂 Permits Required

Restricted Area Permit needed. Book via registered agency + local guide.
