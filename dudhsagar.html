Dudhsagar Waterfall

The Milky Sea of Goa’s Majestic Cascades

Perfect Time to Visit

October to December: Post-monsoon magic with thunderous flow and lush green surroundings at their peak.

Why Visit?

Immerse in one of India’s tallest waterfalls, cascading through dense forests—an adrenaline and nature lover’s dream.

Best Routes

Take the scenic train route from Goa to Kulem or trek through the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary for a raw, immersive experience.

Local Tips

Wear waterproof shoes, start early to avoid crowds, carry snacks & water, and hire local guides for hidden trail gems.
