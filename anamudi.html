🌄 Why Visit Anamudi?

🏞️ Highest in South India

Soaring at 2,695m, Anamudi rules over Kerala’s wild heart.

🐘 Wildlife Haven

Asian elephants, Nilgiri tahr, and rare butterflies call this home.

🌿 UNESCO Vibes

Set in Eravikulam National Park — a biodiversity hotspot like no other.

🚀 How to Visit

📍 Munnar Base

Start from Munnar. Trekking entry via park authorities and guided tours only.

🗓️ Best Time

October to March — post-monsoon magic with mist-kissed trails.

🛂 Eco Permits

Access is limited to protect nature. Book early. Tread lightly.
