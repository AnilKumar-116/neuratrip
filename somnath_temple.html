Why Visit Somnath Temple?

Somnath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, is revered for its history, resilience, and spiritual power. Situated on the Arabian Sea coast, it is a symbol of faith and devotion through centuries of challenges.

When to Visit Somnath Temple?

The best time to visit is from October to March when the climate is cool and pleasant. Festivals like Maha Shivratri and Diwali are celebrated with grand ceremonies and a vibrant spiritual atmosphere.

Route to Somnath Temple

Somnath is accessible via Diu Airport (nearest) and Rajkot Airport. It is well connected by rail and road with major cities in Gujarat and Maharashtra. Local transport options are available from Veraval railway station.
