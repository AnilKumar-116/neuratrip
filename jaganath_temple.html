When to Visit Jagannath Temple?

Route to Jagannath Temple

Puri is accessible by road, rail, and air. The nearest airport is Bhubaneswar Airport (60 km). Frequent trains and buses connect Puri to major cities like Kolkata, Bhubaneswar, and Chennai. Local transport easily takes you to the temple.
