Why Visit Golden Temple?

The Golden Temple, or Harmandir Sahib, is the spiritual heart of Sikhism and a beacon of peace and unity. Its stunning gold-plated exterior and sacred sarovar (holy pool) attract millions seeking tranquility and spiritual solace.

When to Visit Golden Temple?

The best time to visit is between November and March when the weather is cool and pleasant. Key festivals like Vaisakhi and Guru Nanak Jayanti bring vibrant celebrations full of culture and devotion.

Route to Golden Temple

Amritsar is well connected by air, rail, and road. The Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport is just 13 km away. Regular trains and buses link Amritsar to major cities like Delhi, Chandigarh, and Lahore (nearby).
