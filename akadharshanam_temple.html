Why Visit Akshardham Temple?

Akshardham Temple in Delhi is a masterpiece of spiritual and architectural brilliance. It blends art, culture, and devotion, offering exhibitions, a mesmerizing water show, and deep insights into India's spiritual heritage.

When to Visit Akshardham Temple?

Visit from October to March for comfortable weather. Evenings are ideal for witnessing the stunning Sahaj Anand Water Show. Avoid Mondays, as the temple remains closed for maintenance.

Route to Akshardham Temple

Located in East Delhi, the temple is easily accessible via Akshardham Metro Station on the Blue Line. It's well connected by road and is just 30 minutes from Connaught Place and major railway stations.
