Bhagsu Waterfall

Dharamshala’s Sacred Cascade

Perfect Time to Visit

March to June: Crisp weather, clear skies, and perfect conditions for trekking through the lush Himalayan trails.

Why Visit?

Explore a sacred waterfall nestled near the ancient Bhagsunath Temple, surrounded by yoga cafés, artists, and mountain mystique.

Best Routes

Start from Mcleodganj main square; hike 1.5 km through scenic trails. Easily reachable from Dharamshala by road or cab.

Local Tips

Carry a light backpack, wear hiking shoes, take a dip in icy waters, enjoy the cafés, and watch for live music at Shiva Café.
