Jog Falls

India’s Majestic Cascade of Power and Beauty

Perfect Time to Visit

July to October: The monsoon feeds the falls to their thunderous peak, delivering breathtaking views and roaring energy.

Why Visit?

Witness the second highest plunge waterfall in India, split into four distinct cascades — a spectacle of nature’s raw power.

Best Routes

Travel via Sagara town; trek down scenic paths or take the viewpoint access road for panoramic vistas of the roaring falls.

Local Tips

Carry rain gear during monsoon, avoid slippery rocks, visit early morning to catch the misty sunrise, and support local artisans.
