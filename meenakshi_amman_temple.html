Why Visit Meenakshi Amman Temple?

Experience the awe-inspiring Dravidian architecture and vibrant spiritual atmosphere of the Meenakshi Amman Temple, a sacred landmark dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar. It’s a cultural treasure of Madurai.

When to Visit Meenakshi Amman Temple?

The ideal time to visit is from October to March when the climate is cool. Don’t miss the grand Chithirai Festival in April, a spectacular celebration drawing thousands of devotees.

Route to Meenakshi Amman Temple

Madurai is well connected by road, rail, and air. The nearest airport is Madurai Airport, about 12 km away. Regular trains and buses connect Madurai to major cities like Chennai, Bangalore, and Coimbatore.
