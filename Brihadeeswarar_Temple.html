Why Visit Brihadeeswarar Temple?

Brihadeeswarar Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site, showcases the grandeur of Chola architecture. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, it stands as a timeless marvel of craftsmanship, spirituality, and cultural history in Thanjavur.
