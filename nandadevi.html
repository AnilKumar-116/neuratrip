🌄 Why Visit Nanda Devi?

🗻 Sacred Summit

At 7,816m, Nanda Devi is the highest peak within India. A living goddess to locals.

🌿 Biosphere Reserve

UNESCO World Heritage Site with rare Himalayan flora and fauna in full glory.

🚫 Restricted Majesty

Few have entered the Nanda Devi Sanctuary. Experience the untouchable wilderness.

🚀 How to Visit

📍 Trekking Routes

Start from Lata Village to Dharansi Pass. Or explore the outer sanctuary trails.

🗓️ Best Time

May–June and September–October offer the best trekking weather.

🛂 Permits Required

Special permit needed for Nanda Devi National Park. Guided groups only.
