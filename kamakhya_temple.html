Why Visit Kamakhya Temple?

Kamakhya Temple, perched atop Nilachal Hill in Assam, is a revered Shakti Peetha. Dedicated to Goddess Kamakhya, it is a center of tantric worship, symbolizing fertility, power, and the divine feminine energy.

Route to Kamakhya Temple

Located in Guwahati, Assam, Kamakhya is well connected by air, rail, and road. The temple is just 8 km from Guwahati Railway Station and about 20 km from Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport.
