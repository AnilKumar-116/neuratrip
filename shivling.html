🌄 Why Visit Shivling?

🕉️ Sacred Symbol

Named after Lord Shiva’s lingam, this 6,543m peak is revered by sadhus and climbers alike.

🧗 Alpine Perfection

With its sharp spire and sheer faces, it’s a magnet for mountaineers worldwide.

🌌 Gateway to Gaumukh

Begin your trek at the source of the Ganges — myth meets ice at 13,200 ft.

🚀 How to Visit

📍 Trekking Route

Start from Gangotri → Bhojbasa → Tapovan. View Shivling from its sacred southern face.

🗓️ Best Time

Visit in Summer (May–June) or Post-Monsoon (Sep–Oct) for clear skies & spiritual silence.

🛂 Permits Required

Entry pass for Gangotri National Park required. Guides recommended for Tapovan trek.
