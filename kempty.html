Kempty Falls

Mussoorie’s Most Iconic Waterfall Retreat

Perfect Time to Visit

March to June: Escape summer heat, enjoy refreshing dips, and explore lush greenery in full bloom.

Why Visit?

Experience cascading waters in a natural pool setting, perfect for families, swimmers, and thrill-seekers alike.

Best Routes

Located 15 km from Mussoorie; accessible via road, cable car, or even by foot for adventurous hikers.

Local Tips

Arrive early, rent changing rooms, avoid weekends, try street food nearby, and don’t miss the cable ropeway views.
