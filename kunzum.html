Kunzum Waterfall

High-Altitude Beauty of the Spiti Valleys

Perfect Time to Visit

June to September: Snow clears, roads open, and the fall flows with melting glaciers in full force.

Why Visit?

Kunzum’s hidden waterfalls are raw, untouched, and surreal — ideal for true explorers and altitude junkies.

Best Routes

Reach from Manali via Rohtang Pass and Gramphu. A rough ride through Spiti terrain — 4x4 recommended.

Local Tips

Prepare for high altitude, carry oxygen if needed, fuel up early, and combine with a stop at Kunzum La & Chandratal.
