🌄 Why Visit Trishul?

🔱 Symbolic Majesty

Named after Lord Shiva’s trident, Trishul I rises to 7,120m — a divine beacon in snow.

⛏️ Mountaineering History

In 1907, it became the first peak over 7,000m ever climbed. A legend lives here.

🏞️ Kumaon Glory

Witness the full Trishul massif — three peaks dancing above Roopkund and Nanda Devi Sanctuary.

🚀 How to Visit

📍 Trek Route

Approach via Wan → Bedni Bugyal → Roopkund. Best views from Junargali & Brahmatal.

🗓️ Best Time

May–June and Sep–Oct offer the clearest windows into the Trishul skyline.

🛂 Permits Required

Forest entry passes for Bugyals and Nanda Devi biosphere. Guides highly recommended.
