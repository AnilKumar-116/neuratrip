Hogenakkal Falls

The Smoky Thunder of the South

Perfect Time to Visit

October to March: Cooler weather and powerful water flow make it ideal for coracle rides and exploration.

Why Visit?

Dubbed the “Niagara of India,” Hogenakkal offers cascading falls, medicinal baths, and ancient rocky landscapes carved by time.

Best Routes

Drive from Bengaluru (~180 km) or take a bus/train to Dharmapuri, then a cab to the falls. Scenic, smooth, and thrilling.

Local Tips

Try the famous fried fish, hire coracle rides early, bring cash (limited ATMs), and avoid weekends to beat the tourist wave.
