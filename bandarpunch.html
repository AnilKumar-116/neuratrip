🌄 Why Visit Bandarpunch?

🦍 Mythological Roots

Named after Hanuman’s flaming tail. This mountain breathes ancient stories.

🏔️ Glacial Power

Source of the Yamuna. A vital Himalayan watershed with icy serenity.

🥾 Alpine Challenge

Snow-covered peaks, pine-lined routes. A trekker’s high-altitude dream.

🚀 How to Visit

📍 Sankri Gateway

Access via Sankri village in Uttarkashi. Guided treks only. Logistics essential.

🗓️ Best Time

May–June & Sep–Oct. Witness snowfields or golden meadows, your choice.

🛂 Permits

Govt and forest permissions required. Go with eco-certified operators.
