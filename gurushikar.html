🌄 Why Visit Guru Shikhar?

🛕 Sacred Summit

Named after Guru Dattatreya, it’s more than a peak — it’s a spiritual climax.

🌄 Panoramic Majesty

From the top, gaze over the Aravallis — ancient, wise, and weathered.

🕊️ Peace Above All

Far above Mount Abu’s buzz, Guru Shikhar whispers solitude and transcendence.

🚀 How to Visit

🚗 Easy Access

Drive or hike from Mount Abu. A well-paved road leads to the base of the summit.

🗓️ Best Time

Visit between October and March for mist-free sunrises and divine skies.

⛩️ No Permit Needed

No paperwork. Just devotion. Entry to temples is free and open to all.
